Task 4-1: Design of Synchronous Sequential Machines

Design #1 (Mealy machine)[1]:   What assumptions did you make in the design of this machine?

1)      I assumed that when a child was inside the car seat but the car key was within 10 meters and the machine was not in the emergency state, the machine would remain in the same state. I made this assumption as I could not have reset to the first state as the parent could have forgotten about his/her child and was just nearby, the situation was not dangerous enough to elevate the state to the next level.

2)      I assumed that when the baby was inside the car seat but the keys were nearby, and the car was not in an emergency state, a notification would be sent to the parent but the state would not rise. I chose to do this as while the parent could be nearby the car, they could forget about the child.

3)      I created a reset on the machine and the machine resets when the input is at 0.
